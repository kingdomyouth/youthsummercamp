SUMMER CAMP

2 Corinthians 5:21"GOD MADE HIM WHO KNEW NO SIN TO KNOW SIN FOR US, SO IN HIM WE MIGHT BECOME THE RIGHTEOUSNESS OF JESUS"

God is good, God is eternally good!! We where just a bunch of wide eyed adolecent teens that got to experice Gods precence and his never ending love. we rejoyced with the angles, prayed in his presence, and connected our selves with God as we disconnected with the outter world. He was amongst us when we played games, he laughed with us when we told stories, and he loved with us, but the greatest thing about that is that he loved us first. In fact he loved us so much he sent his only begotten son for us so that whosoever believeth in him should not perish, but have everlasting life.

Righteousness

The theme this year was Righteousness through faith in Jesus Christ

Personally, as part of the youth, i have learnt to love more in general, not because i loved God first, but because he loved me first.
